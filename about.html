About Me

Cybersecurity has been an interest of mine since taking a course on it in college. Soft Skills

Learning Agility: Technology is evolving rapidly which requires the ability to learn quickly and adapt to new challenges. This leads to an environment of constant learning opportunities which I enjoy. Change can be difficult but I enjoy learning about new technologies and exploring the ways they can integrate into systems or help make an existing system more efficient.


Teamwork and Leadership: I have participated in sports and group activities since I was very young which has allowed me build strong teamwork skills. I have been in leadership positions as a captain on teams as well as planning a more supporting role in group projects. My diverse experience working in teams has allowed me to gain a sense of how important each part of a team is and how roles that may not appear as overly important often play critical roles in ensuring the overall performance of the team. I have often been looked at to lead projects, both academically as the team leader on assignments and in my career championing projects or bigger assignments.


Communication: Whether written, verbal, or visual communication plays a key role in everyday life and business. I do my best to communicate efficiently and effectively in all types of communication. I believe being an effective communicator is one of the most important parts of business because without effective and clear communication the overall goal or purpose can be lost. I took a professional writing course in college and I believe that really helped me to understand the importance of how to communicate in a business setting.


Positivity: Maintaining a positive outlook even in the face of disappointment or struggle can have a large effect on the ultimate outcome of a situation. While this can sometimes be a challenging endeavor I try to maintain a positive attitude because it helps strengthen relationships and collaboration efforts. I think most people have seen the drain a negative attitude to a situation can cause and often this can lead to an already difficult situation having a longer or worse long term affect. I try to always spread positivity because even a failure endeavor can be seen as a learning opportunity to make the next one better.


Problem Solving: I employ a wide variety of methods to get problems solved as best I can. I am the person many people bring their problems to because they know I will either know how to fix the problem or I am willing to figure out how to solve it. As stated above I treat every opportunity I get as a learning opportunity which has allowed me to grow and develop a wide assortment of skills and knowledge, namely how to do research, so that I can assist with fixing a large variety of issues. I have a passion for solving problems and I think that has lead me to be successful so far in my career as an IT Specialist and before that as a Manager doing repairs on sporting equipment (mostly bicycles but also a wide variety of other equipment).


Dependability: I believe this is a core reason I find working in teams to be successful and often get put into a leadership position. I value being someone that does what they say they will do, if you present me with a problem even if I am unable to rectify the issue myself I will follow up to ensure the problem gets resolved. I also make sure to be where I am supposed to be when I say I will be there; I strive to always be at least a tad bit early but never late. Making sure my work product is consistent and dependable is a key principle to my work performance.
